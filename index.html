FAQs

How does it work?

We use a combination of a specific laser technology and an at-home product to break up the pigment in your vaginal and anal areas.

When Will I See Results?

Most patients are happy with their results after 6 treatments. Treatments are about 4 weeks apart.

Does it hurt?

Not at all! We use a high quality numbing cream. Most patients report that the treatment is very comfortable.

Is it awkward?

Our consultants, providers, and staff are committed to making your experience comfortable and pleasant. Believe it or not, this is a common issue for women in all age groups and skin types. We are here to help you feel your best.

I'm darker skinned. Will it work for me too?

Absolutely. Our highly experienced laser aesthetician will do a test spot to make sure that your skin responds to the program. We have had great success with all skin types, including people with skin like Halle Berry, Mindy Kaling, and Jordan Sparks.

What else will I notice?

Not only will the color lighten, you will also have smoother skin texture, fewer wrinkles, and tighter skin.

Do you guarantee results?

While we do not guarantee a specific result, everyone we have treated so far has seen an improvement.

How do I prepare for my treatment?

We require that you use an at home kojic acid product for two weeks before your first treatment. This helps the melanin in your skin start to break up, and makes your first laser session more impactful.

Why is my vagina getting darker?

There are many reasons the skin gets darker. Hormones, age, shaving regularly, weight gain, and genetics can all cause intimate areas to darken over time.
